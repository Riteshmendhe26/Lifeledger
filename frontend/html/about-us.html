About LifeLedger

LifeLedger is a next-generation organ donation platform built on the power of blockchain technology. Our mission is to revolutionize organ donation by making it more secure, transparent, and efficient. We aim to save lives by connecting donors and recipients through a decentralized, trust-driven network.

Our platform ensures real-time data sharing, traceable organ transport, and legally verifiable smart contracts to prevent fraud and reduce transplant delays. We believe that innovation in technology can drive meaningful change in healthcare.
